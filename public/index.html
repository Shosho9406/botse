Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order? ▼

Simply browse our products on the Shop page, select your preferred colors and customizations, add items to your cart, and click "Send Order to WhatsApp". You'll be taken directly to WhatsApp where you can chat with us to confirm details and arrange payment.

What's the deposit requirement? ▼

We require a 50% deposit upfront to confirm your order and purchase materials. The remaining 50% is due before delivery. This ensures we have the commitment to craft your items and helps us manage materials efficiently.

How long does it take to receive my order? ▼

Production time: 5-10 business days after deposit is received. Delivery: Johannesburg & surrounding areas can be arranged. For areas outside Johannesburg, courier fees apply. We'll confirm delivery options with you via WhatsApp.

Can I customize the colors? ▼

Yes! All products are customizable. You can request specific colors, add patterns, stars, hearts, and other embellishments. Describe what you want in the customization field on the shop page or tell us via WhatsApp. There may be an additional charge for complex custom designs.

Do you offer bulk orders or discounts? ▼

Absolutely! We welcome bulk orders for weddings, events, corporate gifts, and resellers. We offer special pricing for orders of 10+ items. Contact us via WhatsApp to discuss your bulk order needs and get a custom quote.

What payment methods do you accept? ▼

We accept bank transfers and digital payments via Capitec, FNB, Absa, and other South African banks. We'll provide our bank details once you've sent your order via WhatsApp. Payment plans may be available for larger orders — just ask!

What if I'm not happy with my order? ▼

We're committed to quality and customer satisfaction. If there's an issue with your order, contact us immediately via WhatsApp with photos. We'll work with you to resolve the problem — whether that's redoing the item, offering another product, discount on the next order.

Can I request a size/fit guide? ▼

Slouchy Beanies: One size fits most, designed to be loose. Scrunchies: Standard size works for most hair types. Open vs Closed: Open slouchies are more relaxed; Closed are snugger. Ask us via WhatsApp for specific dimensions!
