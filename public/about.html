Our Story

Botse by Masego began as a passion project in 2024, when the founder started crocheting colourful beanies and accessories for friends and family. Word spread quickly, and soon we were shipping products all over South Africa. Each item is handmade with love, using high-quality yarns and attention to detail. We specialise in slouchy beanies, scrunchies and custom designs tailored to your preferences.

What Makes Us Different

  • Handcrafted Quality: No mass production – every piece is made by hand.
  • Custom Options: Choose your colours, patterns, and even add embellishments.
  • Local Business: Proudly South African, with responsive WhatsApp support.
  • Small Batch & Personal: We treat every order with care as if it were for our own family.

Our Mission

We aim to bring joy through colourful, handmade crochet items that feel personal and meaningful. Whether you're buying a gift, treating yourself, or placing a bulk order for an event, we want the experience to be easy, fun, and memorable.
